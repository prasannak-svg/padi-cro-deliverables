🔍 Key CRO Findings
Critical Drop — PADI → Store
Only 9.2% of padi.com users reach the Courses PLP on store.padi.com. Cross-domain handoff is the #1 conversion leak. Desktop converts 2× better than mobile at this step.
ATC Abandonment — Travel Funnel
Travel Add-to-Cart abandonment hits 92.5%. Mobile is worst at 95.1%. Only 5.4% of users who reach the Travel homepage convert to any downstream step.
Prospect Base Confirmed
3.27M clean prospect users on padi.com after removing 315K login sessions. /education/continue-learning kept in funnel — 36K users, accessible to anonymous visitors.
Path Loop: Dive Shops Nearby
495K users engage the nearby tool yet the #1 next step is Homepage (8,903 users) — suggesting low intent completion. Course locator and dive sites are the real downstream signals.
